Waremdi Lake
Waremdi Lake

Biak Numfor Regency in Papua has many natural tourist attractions that are no less beautiful than other areas. Mountains, beaches, and lakes are a series of natural beauty that can be found there. One of them is Telaga Waremdi which is located in Pyefuri village, East Numfor District, Biak Numfor Regency.
Opened since 2017, Telaga Waremdi is one of the hidden gems that not many people know about. This tourist attraction offers the charm of naturally formed springs.
