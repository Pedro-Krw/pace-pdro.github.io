Padaido Island

Padaido Island is a district in Biak Numfor Regency, Papua, Indonesia. The Padaido Archipelago is a group of islands located in the north of Papua. T hese islands also save a lot of underwater natural wealth such as beautiful coral reefs and fish that live around these waters. In addition, in the waters of the Padaido archipelago, there are also remains of remains of warships that occurred in the past. Besides the beauty of the sea, the Padaido Islands also has the beauty of the land and also the wealth of plants such as coconut trees, medicinal plants, and also sago plants.
