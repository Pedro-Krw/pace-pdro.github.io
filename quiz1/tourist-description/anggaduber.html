Anggaduber Description

Angaduber Beach. Not too far from the city of Biak. It only takes 50 minutes to see the white sand that inhabits the long beach. This beach, which is located in East Biak, has an attraction as a place to play water. The colorful coral reefs are tempting to dive while enjoying the colorful fish.
