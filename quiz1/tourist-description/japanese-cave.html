Japanese Caves
Japanese Caves Description

The Japanese Cave is located in the center of Biak City. To go to this cave, can be reached by motorbike or car. The Japanese Cave Manager charges IDR 25 thousand per person if you want to enjoy historical tours in the Japanese Cave.
As soon as you enter the Japanese Cave area, you can see hundreds of remnants of World War II in the front yard. Then, if you look again into the cave, visitors must descend several stairs which are quite steep.
