Sagu Sinole
Sagu Sinole Description

Sagu Sinole is the most unique processed sago because it has a shape like egg crust. This dish is shaped like a rolled pancake, with a thick skin and dense filling. Sinole has the main ingredient of a mixture of sago flour that has been previously sifted, to produce smaller particles.
