Sagu Kering
Sagu Kering Description

DryDried sago has a texture resembling a biscuit made from sago flour. This dish has a square shape and protrudes slightly at the top of the bread. Dried sago has a rough texture, so Papuans usually consume it by soaking dried sago in sweet tea to soften the texture.
