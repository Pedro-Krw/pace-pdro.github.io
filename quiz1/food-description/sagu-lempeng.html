Sagu Lempeng
Sagu Lempeng Description

Sagu Lempeng is a typical Papuan food that has a plate or flat shape. In Papua itself, plate sago is known as Papuan white bread made from sago, then molded, and baked. Plate sago is very suitable to be used as a friend to drink coffee or tea at Santa time
