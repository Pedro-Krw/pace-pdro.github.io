Ikan Kuah Kuning
Ikan Kuah Kuning Description

Ikan kuah bumbu kuning is a typical food of Papua and Maluku whose main ingredient is tuna. The taste of this dish is fresh sour with a yellow color thanks to the use of turmeric. Yellow gravy fish is suitable for a family meal because it is easy to make.
