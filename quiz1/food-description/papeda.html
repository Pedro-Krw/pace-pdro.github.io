Papeda
Papeda Description

Papeda is a food in the form of sago porridge typical of Papua and Maluku which is usually served with tuna or mubara fish seasoned with turmeric. Papeda is white and has a sticky texture resembling glue with a bland taste. Papeda is a food that is rich in fiber, low in cholesterol, and quite nutritious.
